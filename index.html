About Me

Hello and thank you for visiting my portfolio! My name is Zeus I'm in my early 30's and live in the Tampa Bay area with my long time girlfriend, 3 cats and our dog.
I have been interested in computer programing and software engineering for many years now but have never been able to dedicate enough time really do anything with it.
I started working in craft breweries shortly after I turned 21 and became the general manager of a brewery by the time I was 24, I spent the next several years focusing most of my time on my work and really did enjoy the company I worked for and the people I worked with in that time. Unfortunately though I didn't feel like there was anywhere else to grow within that industry and decided this was the time to make a change.
